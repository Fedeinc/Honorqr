Frequently Asked Questions

What is HonorQR?

HonorQR integrates technology with traditional memorials, offering a dynamic and interactive way to remember loved ones. Our platform provides digital memorials that preserve the legacy of your loved ones with photos, videos, and stories.

How does the QR code work?

The QR code is a unique identifier that links the physical memorial to a personalized digital profile. Scanning the QR code with a smartphone or tablet directs you to the digital memorial page where you can view and interact with the content.

What features are included in the digital memorials?

Our digital memorials include features such as personalized profiles, photo and video galleries, an interactive tribute wall, audio memories, multilingual support, and more. Each feature is designed to help you preserve and celebrate the memories of your loved ones.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, HonorQR offers a user-friendly mobile app that allows you to access and interact with memorials on-the-go. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices.

How much does it cost to use HonorQR?

We offer several pricing plans to suit your needs, starting from 59 Rands per year. Each plan includes different features and levels of support. You can find more details on our Prices page.

How do I get started with HonorQR?

Getting started with HonorQR is easy. Simply sign up on our website, choose a pricing plan that suits your needs, and follow the instructions to create a digital memorial for your loved one. If you need any assistance, our support team is here to help.
