    How It Works

    Step 1: Create an Account

    To get started, you need to create an account on HonorQR. This will allow you to access all the features and services we offer. Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details.

    Step 2: Set Up Your Memorial Profile

    Once you have an account, you can set up a memorial profile for your loved one. Add their name, photos, videos, and a biography to create a beautiful tribute. You can also invite friends and family to contribute.

    Step 3: Generate and Place QR Code

    HonorQR generates a unique QR code for each memorial profile. Print this QR code and place it on a headstone, plaque, or any other significant location. Visitors can scan the QR code with their smartphones to view the memorial profile.

    Step 4: Engage with Interactive Tribute Wall

    The interactive tribute wall allows friends and family to post messages, share memories, and upload photos and videos. This creates a dynamic and living tribute that evolves over time.

    Step 5: Access Grief Support Resources

    HonorQR offers a variety of grief support resources, including articles, videos, and links to support groups. These resources are designed to help you and your loved ones through the grieving process.

    Step 6: Manage Your Account

    You can manage your account settings, update your profile, and track contributions from friends and family. HonorQR makes it easy to keep everything organized and up-to-date.
